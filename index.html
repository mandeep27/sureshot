About Us Sureshotgps™ was been developed by a team of engineers

Sureshotgps™ was been developed by a team of engineers and international sales professionals to meet the challenge of measuring distances to the hazards and green from anywhere on the golf course, without having to look for physical markers. The team had a background in electronics and RF (radio frequency) design, encryption (ensuring the security of the data used with the Sureshotgps™), worldwide sales distribution all in the technology industry.
